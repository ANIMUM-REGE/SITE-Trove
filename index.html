Common questions

Where is my data? On your iPhone, inside Trove, included in your normal iPhone backup. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

How do I keep a copy safe off my phone? Open the Report tab and export a PDF or CSV to Files, email, or your cloud drive. Trove will remind you when your records have grown since the last export.

How do I cancel my subscription? Subscriptions are managed by Apple: Settings → your name → Subscriptions.
